Criniti's Kotara

Italian Restaurant in Kotara

Criniti’s is the best Italian restaurant in Kotara, serving authentic Italian flavours in the Western Suburbs. Conveniently located at Westfield Kotara, The Rooftop, CNR Park Ave, and Northcott DR, our restaurant offers an ideal venue to celebrate with family and friends. Our menu features more than 300 items, including the best-woodfired pizzas, ribs, pasta, seafood, grilled fish, bruschetta, risotto, and more, combining Southern Italian and contemporary Australian cuisine.

At Criniti’s, we take pride in serving the freshest ingredients, cooked to perfection, and served with a smile. Whether you’re in the mood for a quick bite or a leisurely meal, our menu caters to all tastes and dietary requirements. From classic pizzas to hearty pasta dishes, you’ll find something you love at Criniti’s.

Don’t miss the opportunity to experience the best Italian restaurant in Kotara. Visit us at Criniti’s Kotara and indulge in the flavours of Italy. Make a reservation now at crinitis.com.au and check out our menu to explore our delicious offerings. You can also find us on Google Maps for directions to our location.

As the best Italian restaurant in Kotara, we also offer an ideal event location for your function. Our famous 1-meter, 2 meters, and 3 meters woodfired pizzas are prepared using our secret recipe dough and only the freshest ingredients. Our menu also features authentic Italian antipasti, ribs, steaks, burgers, gourmet salads, great cocktails, and an impressive selection of wines. And, of course, no meal at Criniti’s would be complete without one of our authentic Italian classic desserts.

The ambiance of our restaurant is perfect for any occasion, group bookings, or corporate events. Celebrating a birthday in Kotara or Celebrating an event in Kotara, don’t worry. Criniti’s got you covered. Head to our website, crinitis.com.au, or BOOK NOW to secure your party. At Criniti’s, our set menus cover a range of food, dietary requirements, and unlimited refills* while you celebrate your event at Criniti’s Kotara.

Kotara is a suburb located in Newcastle, and it is home to one of Criniti’s restaurant locations. The Kotara Criniti’s is situated at the rooftop in Westfield Kotara, a large shopping center in the area. The restaurant has both indoor and outdoor seating areas, and the decor is modern and stylish.
