Criniti's Carousel

Italian Restaurant in Carousel

Criniti’s is the best Italian restaurant in Carousel, serving authentic Italian flavours in Perth. Conveniently located at Westfield Carousel, R211/1382 Albany Highway, Cannington, WA 6107, our restaurant offers an ideal venue to celebrate with family and friends. Our menu features more than 300 items, including the best-woodfired pizzas, ribs, pasta, seafood, grilled fish, bruschetta, risotto, and more, combining Southern Italian and contemporary Australian cuisine.

At Criniti’s, we take pride in serving the freshest ingredients, cooked to perfection, and served with a smile. Whether you’re in the mood for a quick bite or a leisurely meal, our menu caters to all tastes and dietary requirements. From classic pizzas to hearty pasta dishes, you’ll find something you love at Criniti’s.

Don’t miss the opportunity to experience the best Italian restaurant in Carousel. Visit us at Criniti’s Carousel and indulge in the flavours of Italy. Make a reservation now at crinitis.com.au and check out our menu to explore our delicious offerings. You can also find us on Google Maps for directions to our location.

As the best Italian restaurant in Carousel, we also offer an ideal event location for your function. Our famous 1-meter, 2 meters, and 3 meters woodfired pizzas are prepared using our secret recipe dough and only the freshest ingredients. Our menu also features authentic Italian antipasti, ribs, steaks, burgers, gourmet salads, great cocktails, and an impressive selection of wines. And, of course, no meal at Criniti’s would be complete without one of our authentic Italian classic desserts.

The ambiance of our restaurant is perfect for any occasion, group bookings, or corporate events. Celebrating a birthday in Carousel or Celebrating an event in Carousel, don’t worry. Criniti’s got you covered. Head to our website, crinitis.com.au, or BOOK NOW to secure your party. At Criniti’s, our set menus cover a range of food, dietary requirements, and unlimited refills* while you celebrate your event at Criniti’s Carousel.

Westfield Carousel is a large shopping centre located in the suburb of Cannington and features a range of stores and amenities. The centre also has a variety of food and beverage options, from fast food outlets to sit-down restaurants and cafes. We are situated just at the right spot at the rooftop dining precinct.
