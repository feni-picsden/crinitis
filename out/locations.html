find a criniti's near you

Welcome to Criniti’s – the leading restaurant chain serving the best of Italian cuisine with a contemporary Australian twist. Our menu features mouth-watering wood-fired pizzas, pasta, seafood, grilled fish, bruschetta, risotto, ribs, and more. With multiple locations across the country, including Carlton, Southbank, Perth, Castle Hill, Parramatta (next to Bay Vista), Brighton Le Sand, Wetherill Park, and 5 more locations to come this year, we offer a unique dining experience at each of our locations.

At Criniti’s, we use only the freshest ingredients and our secret dough recipe to bring you the best wood-fired pizzas in town. Whether you’re looking for a quick bite or a leisurely meal, we have something for everyone. Our menu also includes authentic Italian antipasti, steaks, burgers, gourmet salads, cocktails, and an impressive wine selection.

Celebrate any occasion with family and friends at Criniti’s – the perfect venue for your event. And don’t forget to finish off your meal with one of our authentic Italian classic desserts. Come visit us at any of our locations and experience the taste of Italy right here in Australia.

Keep an eye out for new Criniti’s locations coming soon!

This is why at Criniti’s we say, when you’re here, you’re family…salute!
