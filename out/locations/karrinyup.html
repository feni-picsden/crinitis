Criniti's Karrinyup

Italian Restaurant in Karrinyup

Criniti’s Italian Cuisine has arrived at Karrinyup Shopping CentreÂ 

Criniti’s, the beloved Italian dining experience known for its authentic and delicious dishes, has opened its doors in the Karrinyup Shopping Centre on December 6th. Featuring a menu filled with the best of Italian cuisine, Criniti’s will provide a delightful culinary journey for all its guests.

From traditional Italian pizzas to fresh, handmade pasta, and from succulent seafood to mouth-watering desserts, Criniti’s brings the essence of Italy straight to your table. Their dishes are prepared using the freshest ingredients and offer a taste of Italian tradition mixed with innovation, making every meal a unique experience.

Notably, Criniti’s was voted Australia’s Favourite Italian in the I Love Food Awards, and their opening at Karrinyup is eagerly anticipated. The new location promises to continue the tradition of excellence set by its predecessors, with a warm and inviting ambiance perfect for family gatherings, date nights, or just a relaxing meal after a day of shopping.

Bookings are now open, and guests are encouraged to reserve their tables to experience the culinary wonders that Criniti’s offers. Don’t miss the opportunity to embark on an Italian culinary journey like no other!

For more information, to view the menu, or to book a table, please visit Criniti’s Website and La Famiglia
